\section*{Findings}

\subsection*{Definition Consistency}
All terms (\(g_{\mu\nu}\), \(F_{\mu\nu}\), \(S_{\text{gravity}}\), \(S_{\text{gauge}}\), \(\rho_{\text{link},G}\), \(\ell_f\), \(g_G\)) are consistently defined according to the SAT\_O Glossary. No undefined symbols or ambiguities were found.

\subsection*{Logical Structure Validation}
The module logically derives the emergent metric tensor and gauge fields from the topology of filamentary structures. The sequence of derivations is logically sound and free of circular reasoning.

\subsection*{Mathematical Correctness}

\subsubsection*{Gravity Sector}
The gravitational action:
\[
S_{\text{gravity}} = \frac{1}{16\pi G} \int d^4x \sqrt{-g} R
\]
is dimensionally consistent:
\[
[G] = \text{m}^3 \, \text{kg}^{-1} \, \text{s}^{-2}, \quad [R] = \text{m}^{-2}, \quad [d^4x] = \text{m}^4
\]
leading to correct action dimensions in natural (Planck) units.

\subsubsection*{Gauge Sector}
The non-Abelian gauge action:
\[
S_{\text{gauge}} = -\frac{1}{4g_G^2} \int d^4x \sqrt{-g} \, \text{Tr}(F_{\mu\nu} F^{\mu\nu})
\]
with:
\[
F_{\mu\nu} = \partial_\mu A_\nu - \partial_\nu A_\mu + [A_\mu, A_\nu]
\]
is verified with:
\[
\text{Tr}(F_{\mu\nu} F^{\mu\nu}) = F_{\mu\nu}^a F^{\mu\nu,a}
\]
ensuring gauge invariance and correct tensor contractions.

\subsubsection*{Unified Action}
The unified action:
\[
S_{\text{unified}} = S_{\text{gravity}} + S_{\text{gauge}}
\]
is a sum of dimensionally consistent actions.

\subsection*{Group-Theoretical Integrity}
The Lie algebra structure:
\[
[T^a, T^b] = i f^{abc} T^c
\]
and the definition of \(F_{\mu\nu}\) ensure correct non-Abelian gauge invariance without anomalies at the classical level.
