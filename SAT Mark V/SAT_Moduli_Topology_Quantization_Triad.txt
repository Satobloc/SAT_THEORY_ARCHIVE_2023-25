
SAT MODULI–TOPOLOGY–QUANTIZATION TRIAD
======================================

I. STRUCTURE OF THE TRIAD
--------------------------
Topology --> Moduli Space --> Quantization --> Spectra & Observables

Each SAT sector passes through this pipeline to produce quantized, testable structures.

II. θ₄ SECTOR: ANGULAR SCALAR FIELD
------------------------------------
- Topology:
  - Maps θ₄: M → S¹/Z₃
  - Classified by π₀(S¹/Z₃) = ℤ₃
  - Topological charge: Q = (3/2π)[θ₄(∞) − θ₄(−∞)] mod 3

- Moduli:
  - Domain wall class: ℤ₃
  - Kink moduli: position X₀ ∈ ℝ
  - Moduli space: ℳ_θ₄^(k) ≅ ℝ

- Quantization:
  - Wavefunction on kink position
  - Fluctuation spectrum, zero modes, soliton mass

III. τ SECTOR: ℤ₃ DISCRETE GAUGE FIELD
---------------------------------------
- Topology:
  - A ∈ C¹(M, ℤ₃), with δA = 0
  - Equivalence: A ∼ A + δΛ
  - Flat bundles: H¹(M, ℤ₃)

- Moduli:
  - Discrete set of flat gauge sectors
  - On torus: ℳ_τ = ℤ₃ × ℤ₃

- Quantization:
  - Path integral over flat bundles
  - Optional cocycle twist: ω₃ ∈ H³(ℤ₃, U(1))
  - Discrete partition function

IV. θ₄–τ COUPLED SECTOR
-------------------------
- Topology:
  - Coupling term: A ∪ dθ₄
  - Composite topological class: (Δθ₄, [A]) ∈ ℤ₃ × H¹(M, ℤ₃)

- Moduli:
  - Fiber bundle over kink moduli
  - ℳ_SAT = ⨆ₖ (ℝ × ℱₖ), ℱₖ ⊆ H¹(M, ℤ₃)

- Quantization:
  - Wavefunctions: Ψₖ(X₀, [A])
  - Bound state spectrum, multiplet structure
  - Selection rules from topological sectors

V. u^μ SECTOR: TIME FOLIATION FIELD
------------------------------------
- Topology:
  - Homotopy class of unit timelike vector fields
  - Foliation geometry (non-integrable)

- Moduli:
  - Constraint: u^μ u_μ = −1
  - Space of constrained sections modulo diffeos

- Quantization:
  - Dirac bracket quantization
  - Suppressed dynamical modes
  - Observables: energy flux, anisotropy
